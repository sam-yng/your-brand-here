Brand studies

New ideas, made properly.

Each study starts with a different fictional brief — a furniture atelier, a video game launch, a clothing label — and follows it through art direction, interaction, and production code. Three worlds, one standard.

Self-directed concepts — not commissioned client work, and labelled that way on every page.
